Instructions

Nimble is based on the ancient logical game of Nim. Counters are taken by two opponents in turn. Whoever takes the final counter is the winner. You can take up to three counters in your turn, but you must take one - play then passes to the opponent.

Playing

Click button up to three times in your turn. You must click at least once. Click button to pass. Take the final counter to win.
